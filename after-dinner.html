AFTER DINNER SPEAKER

Hugh "Shuggie" Naismith

I decided after reaching the top of my game as a mobile DJ and presenter that I would hone my other talents. Way back in 1999 whilst speaking at my first Spokemen's Dinner in Armadale Bowling Club I was discovered by the talented song writer, comedian and after dinner wit Frank Rob who introduced me onto the After Dinner circuit.

I am now estbalished as one of West Lothian's best After Dinner speakers and comics, telling jokes and sharing stories of my upbringing in my parent's pub and the fun of being one of twin boys. I have hilarious stories and material about my life and times in the pub and some of the characters of the small miner's village of Blackridge, where i grew up and still live toady with my own family.

I have worked with the legendary Frank Carson, Andy Cameron and the late Matt Mcginn, comic and folk singer, who was a close family friend and started me on the road to entertaining audiences. Matt used to teach me his world famous jokes, stories and songs then get me to perform them in the pub when I was just a small boy. I have also spoken alongside many famous sportsmen, past and present, from wide ranging sports including football, golf and rugby.
