Hugh Naismith founded Roadunnner Discotheques back in 1997 when he took his show on the road after spending many years learning his trade in his parent't pubs. At the tender age of just 17 he started out catering for small local discos and parties and is now, over two decades later, his name and his company are well established in central Scotland as one of the biggest and best around.
