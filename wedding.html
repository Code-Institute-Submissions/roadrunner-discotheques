BESPOKE WEDDING PACKAGES

Roadrunner Discotheques aim to make your wedding a magical experience. We pride ourselves on great customer service and always delivering what you, the bride and groom, want. Whether it's music for your wedding ceremoney, background music throughout the wedding breakfast or exciting, professional entertainment for your wedding disco, we can provide exactly what you want to complete your dream day.

An important part of the wedding package is meeting up with the bride and groom to discuss all of your need. We can play music to your theme, help you come up with ideas for request lists and that all important first dance and provide a great lighting set up. The preparation Roadrunner Discotheques wil put in will ensure that your day will live long in the memories of everyone lucky enough to join you. The pre-wedding preparation include liaising with the venue manager to confirm all arrangements.
