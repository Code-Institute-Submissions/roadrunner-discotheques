RACE NIGHTS

The enjoyment of a day at the racetrak brought to a venue of your choice. A race night is an event where participants place money on the outcome of recorded races. The takings of a race night, after expenses of the entertainment, must be devoted to purposes other than private gain.

Race nights are great way to raise funds for your chosen charity and they are great fun too. Why not incorporate a race night in to your corporate event? At Roadrunner Discotheques we supply all the equipment required to run a race night: the races, big screen TV's, projector, sound system, tote tickets, rosettes and a professional race night compere.
